# Editors

## Purpose

Editors are the program maintainers who keep the incubator consistent.

## Scope

Use this page for people who manage the program, criteria, templates,
and stage transitions.

## Responsibilities

- maintain the stage criteria
- keep templates and docs aligned
- assign or confirm reviewers
- approve stage transitions
- keep naming and structure consistent

## Editor rule

Editors should preserve the checklist-based model and avoid adding
unnecessary process.

## Decision standard

Editors make the final call when the checklist and due diligence are
complete enough to support a transition.
