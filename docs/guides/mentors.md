# Mentors

## Purpose

Mentors help authors close gaps without taking over review or approval.

## Scope

Use this page for advisory support during application, review, and
iteration.

## Responsibilities

- guide improvements
- help interpret criteria
- suggest a next step or roadmap item
- help authors translate feedback into action

## Mentor rule

Mentors advise, not control.

They should not replace reviewers or editors.
