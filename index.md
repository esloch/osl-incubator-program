# OSL Incubator

The OSL Incubator supports open source projects from early‑stage ideas to mature, sustainable software.

It acts as a **pre‑incubation layer**, helping projects align with ecosystem standards such as:

* CNCF (cloud‑native)
* pyOpenSci (Python scientific)
* rOpenSci (R ecosystem)

## Program Stages

* **POC** – early‑stage, experimental
* **Incubation** – stabilization and structure
* **Graduation** – maturity and adoption

## Apply

Projects are submitted via GitHub:

[https://github.com/esloch/osl-incubator-program/issues](https://github.com/esloch/osl-incubator-program/issues)

Full criteria and workflow are available in the repository.
