# Lifecycle

OSL follows a simple three‑stage model:

## POC

Early‑stage projects with a working prototype.

## Incubation

Projects improving quality, documentation, and governance.

## Graduation

Projects with stability, adoption, and sustainability.

---

### Workflow

Apply → Review → Feedback → Accept → Iterate → Promote → Evaluate

All transitions are checklist‑based and asynchronous.
